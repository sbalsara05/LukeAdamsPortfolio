Luke Adams

My name is Luke Adams and I’m a 17 year old artist from Westerly, Rhode Island. I am just beginning my journey as an artist and hope to pursue a career in it. My work so far has covered mediums such as pencil, colored pencil, charcoal, oil, watercolor, acrylic, digital, photography, and collage. I hope to be able to share my work with others while also collaborating with them and making art based on their wants. Please take a look at the gallery below and reach out in the section below if you are interested in buying any of the pieces you see or would like to commission me to do a piece personal to you.
